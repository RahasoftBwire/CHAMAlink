1. Introduction

Bwire Finance Cloud ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our digital chama management platform.

2. Information We Collect

Personal Information
  • Full name and username
  • Email address and phone number
  • Date of birth and age verification
  • National ID or passport number
  • Guardian information (for minors)
  • Profile photographs
Financial Information
  • Chama membership details
  • Contribution and payment history
  • Loan applications and approvals
  • M-Pesa transaction details
  • Banking and payment information
Technical Information
  • IP address and device information
  • Browser type and version
  • Login attempts and timestamps
  • Usage patterns and preferences

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain our chama management services
  • Process payments and financial transactions
  • Verify your identity and prevent fraud
  • Send notifications and important updates
  • Comply with legal and regulatory requirements
  • Improve our services and user experience
  • Provide customer support

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We may share your information with:

Service Providers
  • M-Pesa and Safaricom for payment processing
  • Email service providers for notifications
  • Cloud storage providers for data hosting
  • Identity verification services
Legal Requirements
  • Government agencies when required by law
  • Law enforcement for fraud investigation
  • Regulatory bodies (Central Bank of Kenya, etc.)
  • Tax authorities for compliance purposes
Chama Members
  • Basic profile information within your chama
  • Contribution and payment status
  • Meeting attendance records
  • Financial reports and analytics

5. Data Security

We implement robust security measures including:

  • SSL encryption for all data transmission
  • Strong password requirements and account lockouts
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Secure cloud infrastructure
  • Limited access to personal data
  • Employee training on data protection

6. Data Retention

We retain your information:

  • For as long as your account is active
  • For 30 days after account cancellation (for reactivation)
  • For 7 years for financial records (regulatory requirement)
  • Indefinitely for fraud prevention and legal compliance
  • Until you request deletion (where legally permissible)

7. Your Rights

Under Kenyan data protection law, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Restrict processing of your data
  • Data portability
  • Object to data processing
  • Withdraw consent at any time

8. Email Communications

We send emails for:

  • Account verification and security alerts
  • Subscription and payment confirmations
  • Loan approval notifications
  • Meeting reminders and chama updates
  • System maintenance and important updates

Email notifications are sent from: flowbyte.erp25@gmail.com

9. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Maintain your login session
  • Remember your preferences
  • Analyze usage patterns
  • Improve user experience
  • Prevent fraud and abuse

10. Third-Party Services

Our platform integrates with:

  • M-Pesa: For payment processing
  • Safaricom: For SMS and payment services
  • Google Services: For email and analytics
  • Cloud Providers: For hosting and storage

These services have their own privacy policies which govern their use of your information.

11. Minors and Guardian Consent

For users under 18:

  • Parental or guardian consent is required
  • Guardian information is collected and verified
  • Enhanced privacy protections apply
  • Special approval processes for financial transactions
  • Guardian notification for significant activities

12. International Data Transfers

Your data is primarily stored in Kenya. Where international transfers are necessary (e.g., cloud services), we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place including:

  • Adequate data protection frameworks
  • Contractual data protection clauses
  • Compliance with international standards

13. Data Breach Response

In the event of a data breach, we will:

  • Notify affected users within 72 hours
  • Report to relevant authorities as required
  • Implement immediate containment measures
  • Conduct thorough investigation
  • Provide support and guidance to affected users

14. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes via email and through our platform. Your continued use of our services after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
